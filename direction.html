What is Project: New Lebanon?

Project: New Lebanon is a collaborative based community of Lebanese Responsible Citizens looking for answers.

Our Mission

1. Start a constructive dialogue between Us about the future and define what we want

2. Research, compile and curate data about the Opposition Political Actors and their proposed Visions and Plans

3. Transform data into comprehensive information and share it with Lebanese Citizens.

Our Goals

Define and agree on a new Social Contract.

Explore and understand the proposed plans.

Enable other Citizens to take informed decisions in any upcoming elections.

How?

Project: New Lebanon consists of many sub-projects, each with an objective.

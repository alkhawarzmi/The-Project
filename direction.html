What is Project: New Lebanon?

Project: New Lebanon is a community based platform of Lebanese Responsible Citizens looking for answers.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the Citiziens and the opposition Political Actors, by spreading political awareness in an easy, friendly, simplified format, enabling the Citizens to take informed decisions in any upcoming elections, through unbiased, reliable information, insights, tools, and dialogue.

Project: New Lebanon is a platform created, managed and funded by a community of Lebanese Citizens, not Politicians.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the Citiziens and the opposition Political Actors, by spreading political awareness in an easy, friendly and simplified format.

Our goal is to inform andenable Lebanese Citizens, residents or Diaspora, to take informed decisions in any upcoming elections, through unbiased, reliable information, insights, tools, and dialogue.

Project Description Objectives Status Readiness Percentage
Vision, Values and Principle Through the website, Lebanese Citizens can take a survey and provide feedback. The survey is a first draft for a dynamic Vision for Lebanon, along with a set of Values and Principles. Used as a starting point to establish new "Social Contract".
  1. Establishes a baseline for the dialogue; Person to Person, Person to Group, Group to Group; Person to Person, Person to Group, Group to Group
  2. Opens a continuous, uninterrupted dialogue through web forums
  3. Grows by community referrals
  4. Collects Feedback
  5. Enables us to improve
  6. Offers Politicians and Political Parties a better understanding of the Citizens' aspirations.
  7. Allows US "to Flip the Game"
 Ongoing 90%
Political Actors Database Accessible through the website, Lebanese Citizens can browse and read about the political actors, and find them by Political Position, Direction, Plans, Activities and Events.
  1. Establishes one point of reference
  2. Introduces all new political actors, Organizations and People.
  3. Continuous monitoring and updated information
  4. Satisfaction Polls
 Ongoing 75%
Plans for Lebanon Database A normalised database of all published plans, studies and reports for the recovery of Lebanon from different sources, with a digitzed copy of all SWOT analysises and Proposed Strategies and objectives.
  1. Offers the information in a simpler and more accessible way
  2. Enables a One Master plan view combining all plans
  3. Maps SWOT and Srategies to the Vision's goals
  4. View short, mid and long term plans
  5. Experts votes on proposed strategies
 On Hold 10%

Mailing List A daily, weekly and monthly newsletter digest of Past, Current and Upcoming Events from aggregated sources from Alternative Media.
  1. Spread reliable news and updates about the Opposition's activities
  2. Optimize our "phone" times
  3. Reduces our internet consumption

Personal Political Quiz Are you Left or Right? Totalitarian or Libertarian? Religious or Secular? A Lebanese adapted personal political quiz that allows you to see where you stand in the political spectrum compared to fellow citizens and political parties.
  1. Spreads political self awareness
  2. Explains Political positioning and terms
  3. Helps the Citizen find like minded people and political parties
  4. Offers Politicians and Political Parties a better understanding of the Citizens' directions.
